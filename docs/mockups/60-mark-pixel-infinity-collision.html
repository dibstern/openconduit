08 — Concept Note
Infinity = Endless Relay
The figure-8 / infinity symbol represents data flowing continuously through the conduit. Two streams — pink and cyan — travel their respective loops, meeting at the center crossing where they exchange identity. This is the relay moment: data arrives as one thing, gets transformed, and continues as another.

The pixel grid treatment keeps the mark technical and precise. The 2-block-wide strokes give it substance at small sizes. The wider 14×8 aspect ratio naturally accommodates the horizontal figure-8 while remaining compact enough for inline use.
Pink enters left loop → clockwise
Cyan enters right loop → clockwise
Meet at center → swap → repeat

Conduit — Mark: Diamond Conduit

A rotated square (diamond orientation, 45°) with a vertical channel cut through the center. The diamond is the housing, the channel is the conduit. Pixel blocks flow upward through the channel. The left half leans pink, the right half leans cyan, split by the vertical conduit. Geometric, bold, distinctive — a shape you’d stamp on hardware — 52 of 52.

Design Intent
The rotated square is instantly recognizable and distinctive. The vertical channel through its center is the conduit — data flowing upward through a bold geometric housing. The left/right split maps naturally to the pink/cyan palette. At small sizes the diamond reads as a strong stamp-like icon.

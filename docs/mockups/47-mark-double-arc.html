06 — Mark Anatomy
Outer Arc — Pink (#FF2D7B)
Stroke: 5px, round linecap, larger radius
Inner Arc — Cyan (#00E5FF)
Stroke: 5px, round linecap, smaller radius
Channel — the conduit
~8–10px gap between arcs where blocks flow
Pixel Blocks — flowing data
Colored blocks travel the arc channel via animateMotion
Arc span: ~220°
Opening faces downward, concentric center point shared
Design Intent
The double arc suggests both wireless signal transmission and a tube cross-section. The channel between the arcs is the conduit — data flowing through a curved pipe. Distinctive at all sizes: two nested curves read clearly even at 16px.

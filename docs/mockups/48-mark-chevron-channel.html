06 — Mark Anatomy
Left Chevron — Pink (#FF2D7B)
Stroke: 5px, round linecap, V-shape pointing right. Sender.
Right Chevron — Cyan (#00E5FF)
Stroke: 5px, round linecap, V-shape pointing right. Receiver.
Channel — the conduit gap
~12–16px gap between chevron tips where data flows
Pixel Blocks — flowing data
Colored blocks travel left→right through the channel via animateMotion
Aspect Ratio: 2:1 (wider than tall)
Horizontal orientation emphasizes directional flow
Design Intent
Two chevrons suggest terminal prompts, data flow arrows, and relay forwarding. The pink chevron sends, the cyan chevron receives — the gap between is the conduit channel. Directional and unmistakably “tech.” Reads clearly from 120px down to 16px.

06 — Mark Anatomy
▶ PINK enters right, clockwise (down)
▶ CYAN enters right, counter-clockwise (up)
★ Collision on LEFT side → color swap → exit right
Pink Cursor — Clockwise
Enters from right side of ring, goes downward
Travels clockwise around the bottom half
After collision: becomes CYAN, exits right
Cyan Cursor — Counter-Clockwise
Enters from right side of ring, goes upward
Travels counter-clockwise around the top half
After collision: becomes PINK, exits right
Collision Point
Left side of ring (opposite entry)
White flash on impact, then color swap
Ring Path — 11×11 Grid
Bresenham circle, ~2 blocks thick
~36 path blocks ordered clockwise from right
Background blocks: dark grey (#27272A)
4-Phase Animation Cycle
Phase 1 (0-40%): Entry — cursors travel inward
Phase 2 (40-50%): Collision — white flash
Phase 3 (50-90%): Bounce — swapped colors exit
Phase 4 (90-100%): Static hold — gradient ring
Static Logo State (Phase 4)
Top half: cyan, bright at right, fading left
Bottom half: pink, bright at right, fading left
Left side: both colors at low brightness
→ Bicolored ring: two streams in orbit

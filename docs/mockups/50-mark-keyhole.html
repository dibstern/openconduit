06 — Mark Anatomy
Capsule Outline — Pink→Cyan gradient
Stroke: 4px, rounded rectangle, gradient top-to-bottom
Vertical Slot — the conduit
~8px wide channel cut through the center, rounded ends
Pixel Blocks — flowing data
Colored blocks travel upward through the vertical slot
Aspect ratio: ~5:7 (w:h)
Taller than wide; reads as keyhole/shield at all sizes
Design Intent
The keyhole shape suggests access, security, and passage. The vertical slot is the conduit — data flowing upward through a guarded channel. More distinctive than generic circles or rings; the tall capsule reads clearly even at 12px wide.

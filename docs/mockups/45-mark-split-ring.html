06 — Mark Anatomy
Left semicircle — Pink arc
Stroke: 6px, linecap: round. Arcs from bottom (6 o'clock) counterclockwise to top (12 o'clock). Represents the input/upstream half of the conduit.
Right semicircle — Cyan arc
Stroke: 6px, linecap: round. Arcs from bottom (6 o'clock) clockwise to top (12 o'clock). Represents the output/downstream half.
Vertical gap — Pixel flow
8–12px gap between arc endpoints. Pixel blocks (alternating pink/cyan) flow upward through the gap. The data stream passing through the pipe cross-section.
Negative space — The conduit
The interior of the ring reads as empty pipe. The split creates visual tension — two halves framing the data flow like parentheses around a value.
Scalability
At 16px, pixel flow is hidden; the mark reads as a split circle icon. At 100px, pixel blocks are visible and animated. The gap width scales proportionally.

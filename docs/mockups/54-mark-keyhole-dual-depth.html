Conduit — Mark: Keyhole Dual Depth

Based on v50’s keyhole mark (tall capsule with vertical slot), but with two streams of pixel blocks flowing in opposite directions through the slot, each with a faded offset shadow stream for depth. Counter-directional flow shows the relay concept; depth shadows make each stream feel dimensional — like blocks floating above the channel surface.

Depth Effect
The depth comes from three factors: (1) shadow layers are offset 2px from their primary streams, (2) shadow layers have reduced opacity (0.22 vs 0.75), and (3) shadow layers animate slower (2.6s vs 1.8s). Together, these create a parallax illusion — primary streams appear to float above the channel surface.

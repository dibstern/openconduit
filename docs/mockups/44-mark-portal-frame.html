06 — Mark Anatomy
Gradient Frame
SVG rounded rect, stroke-only. Linear gradient from pink (top-left) to cyan (bottom-right). Stroke width 4–6px. rx/ry 8 for smooth corners.
Pixel Block Flow
Repeating-linear-gradient with alternating pink & cyan blocks. Flows upward continuously at 2.4s per cycle. Contained by overflow: hidden on the inner channel.
Hollow Center
The channel IS the interior of the frame. No fill on the rect — the portal is defined by its boundary. Pixel blocks are visible only inside.
3:4 Aspect Ratio
36×48 viewBox at logo size. Wider-than-tall like a screen or doorway. Scales proportionally across all sizes from 16px tiny to 120px large.
Glow Breathe
Animated drop-shadow on the SVG frame pulses between subtle and visible. Pink + cyan dual drop-shadows create an atmospheric halo effect.

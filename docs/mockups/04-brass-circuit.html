Pairing in context
Courier Prime meets IBM Plex Mono
The serif detailing in Courier Prime lends warmth and literary authority to headings and the wordmark. It feels authored, not generated. IBM Plex Mono provides clean, neutral body text that stays legible at small sizes while maintaining the monospace character essential to a developer tool. Together they create a tone that is technical but not cold, precise but not sterile.
The hidden luxury is in the type: serifs where you don't expect them, warmth where tools are usually clinical.
